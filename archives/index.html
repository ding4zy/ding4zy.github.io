About

李孜博士是腾讯的资深专家，专注于人工智能驱动的可持续发展和社会创新，包括技术转型和气候变化。他在2020年的新经济智库峰会上获得了“数字化领域最佳研究员（2009-2019年）”奖项。他的著作《在线城市化》被Springer Nature评为关注联合国可持续发展目标（SDGs）的最热门出版物之一。
Dr Li Zi is a Senior Expert at Tencent. He focuses on AIGC-Driven Sustainable Development and Social Innovation, including Technological Transformation and Climate Change. He got a prize“the best Research Fellow (2009-2019) on digitalization” issued at The New Economy Think Tank Summit 2020. His book “Online Urbanization” is awarded the top used publications that concern the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) by Springer Nature.

在此之前，李孜在新加坡国立大学TLI和CDE担任研究员期间带领团队研究数字服务促进亚洲城乡发展的新范式。也曾在麻省理工学院SAP (School of Architecture and Planning) 担任客座讲师，主要研究城市社区数字化治理。
Previously, Mr. Li served as a researcher at the Transportation Logistics Institute (TLI) and the Centre for Design and Environment (CDE) at the National University of Singapore, where he led a team researching new paradigms of digital services that promote urban and rural development in Asia. Additionally, he was a guest critic at the School of Architecture and Planning (SAP) at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), where his main focus was digital governance in urban communities.

李孜先生拥有新加坡国立大学博士学位和荷兰代尔夫特理工大学理学硕士学位。
Mr. Li holds a Ph.D. degree from the National University of Singapore and a Master of Science degree from Delft University of Technology in the Netherlands.
